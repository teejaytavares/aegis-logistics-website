About Aegis

Not Gig Delivery.
Structured
Logistics.

Aegis Logistics LLC is a disciplined, scalable last-mile logistics operator working in partnership with Amazon. We operate from DLI5 Delivery Station in Pontoon Beach, Illinois, serving the Greater St. Louis Metro — and we are building with intention.

We are not "another DSP." We are a professionally managed logistics company structured for multi-site scale, institutional performance standards, and long-term operational excellence.

Currently operating 20 dedicated delivery routes, Aegis is executing Amazon's operational ramp plan to reach 50 routes by November 2026. Every system, hire, and process decision is designed to support that growth without compromising safety, performance, or culture.

Our growth is intentional, structured, and system-driven — ensuring safety, performance, and operational excellence scale together.

Non-Negotiable Standards

Five Operating
Principles

These are not guidelines. They are the standards against which every decision, every hire, and every delivery is measured.

01
Safety Before Speed

We never sacrifice safety for productivity. Every route, every vehicle, every decision begins with risk assessment. Safety is not a compliance requirement — it is a leadership standard.

02
Radical Accountability

Performance is measured. Metrics are reviewed. Coaching is structured. We own our results — individually and collectively — at every level of the organization.

03
Structured Coaching Over Reaction

We develop people through mentorship, scorecards, and one-on-one review. Correction is progressive, documented, and fair — not emotional.

04
Integrity in All Reporting

Transparent communication with Amazon, drivers, vendors, and leadership. No manipulation of metrics. No hiding incidents. No excuses.

05
Scalable Systems Over Personality

Aegis is built on process, not heroics. Route optimization, fleet maintenance, telematics, scorecards, financial discipline. If it cannot scale to 50 routes, it does not belong in the system.
